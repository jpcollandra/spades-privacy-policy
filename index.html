Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Spades By Negnan handles user information. This app is designed for Apple Watch and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Information We Do Not Collect

The app does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Usage or analytics data
  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers

Offline Operation

The Spades Watch Game works entirely offline. It does not connect to external servers, does not require an internet connection, and does not communicate with third-party services.

Children's Privacy

This app is safe for all ages and collects no data from children or adults.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising frameworks, or data collection tools.

Data Sharing

Because no data is collected, no data is shared with anyone.
